Welcome to Ana's Word Cascade Game!

How to Play:
Each Round consists of a series of Tasks.
In each Task, you’ll have a limited amount of time to form words from the letters in the Active Row.
The longer your word and the rarer the letters, the more points you earn! After time runs out, any unused letters are moved into the Bin (pile). Complete all Tasks in a Round to earn a bonus!

Between Rounds:
You’ll enter a Bin-Clearing Phase where each valid word yields 25% of its normal score in points. Clearing the Bin helps keep space free for future Rounds.

Game Over:
The game ends if the Bin fills up completely.
Manage your letters wisely and aim for the highest score!
