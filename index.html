Welcome to Ana's Word Cascade Game!

How to Play:
Each Round consists of a series of Tasks.
You’ll have a limited amount of time to form words from the letters in the Active Row.
Round 1 tasks get 13 seconds each. Round 2 tasks get 12 seconds each... and so on. After time runs out, any unused letters move to the Bin. Complete all Tasks in a Round to earn a bonus!

Between Rounds:
You’ll enter a Bin-Clearing Phase awarding 25% of normal word score for each valid word. Clearing the Bin helps keep space free for future Rounds.

Game Over:
The game ends if the Bin fills up completely.
Manage your letters wisely and aim for the highest score!
