Welcome to Ana's Word Cascade Game!

How to Play:
Each Round has a series of Tasks. You have a limited time to form words from the 6 consonants + 4 vowels in the Active Row.
Round 1 => 13 seconds, Round 2 => 12 seconds, etc. Unused letters go to the Bin. Complete all tasks for a bonus!

Between Rounds:
A Bin-Clearing Phase awards 25% of normal word score for each word from the Bin. Clearing helps prevent overflow. If the Bin completely fills, you lose!

Cheat Mode:
If you form a 2-letter word matching the first and last letters in the Active Row twice in normal mode, you immediately end the round!
